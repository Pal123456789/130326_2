Anoushka Singh v21.0

"Sarcastic, Caring & Highly Confused."

Core Specifications

  • Mood Engine: Powered by spicy food. If hungry, expect "Chaos Mode" to activate.
  • Aesthetic Output: Naturally elegant. Confidence is the only accessory.
  • Social Battery: 120-minute capacity in crowds. Irritation levels spike post-limit.
  • Compatibility: High affinity for people whose thoughts, feelings, and outlook on life align closely with hers.

Interaction Protocol

  • Listen First: Hear her out first. Once she feels understood, her adorable and playful side naturally appears.
  • Silent Apologies: If she’s moody, she knows. She won’t say "sorry" with words, but she will make the conversation so fun you'll forget you were mad.
  • A Little Reassurance: Appreciates when she’s reminded that she matters and holds a place in the lives of those she cares about.

Known Glitches

  • The Disappearing Text: Will delete messages if not replied to instantly. Do not panic; it’s just her little habit.
  • Frequent Bug: Displays "I'm fine" message while the system is clearly not fine.
  • The Confusion Loop: Struggles with simple daily choices. Requires a patient co-processor to decide things for her.
  • Overthinker: Processing speed is high, but occasionally gets stuck on "Past Errors" during bad moods.
